# Project Vision
## 352 Legends Coaching Platform

*The platform I built for my 352 Legends team — the product itself will get its own name for public release. "352 Legends" is the youth football program I coach for, not a brand I own.*

---

I built this because my two boys were on a winless team, and it broke my heart.

I'm a disabled vet and a single dad. Last season was rough. The kids showed up, they worked hard, but we had no real way to track progress or show them they were actually getting better. I got tired of guessing. Tired of watching their effort disappear at the end of the season with nothing to show for it.

So I started building the tools I wished we had.

What began as a simple coaching manual for our team grew into a full platform. I'm not a professional programmer. I'm just a dad and coach who refused to accept that this was the best we could do for our kids.

This is what I've built so far — and what I'm working toward.

## What It Is Today

352 Legends is a complete coaching platform for youth sports. It's built to be run by real volunteer coaches and leagues, not big companies.

Right now it can do:

- Full multi-team support (one install runs an entire league)
- Training Log with effort & attitude tracking + coaching phases
- Video-verified Combine testing (Vertical, Broad Jump, Shuttle, 40-yard dash)
- Pro Scout pose analysis with skeleton overlays
- Game Day tools — live scoreboard, draggable whiteboard, lineup builder
- Player development tracking with real stats and progress charts
- Parent and player portals (they can actually see their own data)
- Coaching manual, drills, and practice plan builder

Everything is free. Always. No subscriptions. You can run it yourself on a laptop or cheap server.

## Why I'm Building This

Parents are tired of checking five different apps and Facebook groups.

Coaches are tired of losing all their data when a season ends.

Kids deserve to look back one day and see how much they actually improved — not just their final record.

I want this to be the one place where everything lives. Training. Combine numbers. Game film analysis. Progress over years. All of it in one spot, private, and actually useful.

## The Bigger Dream

I want every volunteer coach and every parent in small towns like mine to have better tools than what we had last season.

I want kids to walk away from youth sports with something real — a record of their hard work that actually means something.

And I want to give this platform away. Not sell it. Not turn it into another SaaS company. Just make it available for any league or school that wants it.

That's why I'm open sourcing it. If someone else can make it better, I want them to. If another coach wants to run it for their team, they should be able to. No paywalls. No lock-in.

## Where We Are Right Now

It's already helping my two boys during combine prep. We're on day 8+ of conditioning and I can actually see their endurance patterns and recovery. That alone makes it worth it.

There's still a lot to do — better parent experience, mobile polish, easier setup for other leagues, stronger privacy tools — but the foundation is solid and in daily use.

## Final Words

This didn't come from a tech company.

It came from a dad who was tired of watching his kids' hard work disappear.

If you're a coach, parent, developer, or league admin who believes kids deserve better tools, you're welcome here.

The code is open. The mission is simple.

For the kids.
